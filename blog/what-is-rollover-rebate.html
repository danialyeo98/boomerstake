What Is Rollover Rebate & How Does It Work?

May 2025 · BOOMERSTAKE AUS TEAM

Rollover rebate is one of the most valuable — and most misunderstood — bonuses in online gaming. Here's a simple breakdown.

What Is a Rollover Rebate?

A rollover rebate is a percentage of your total bets returned to you as cash — regardless of whether you win or lose. It's calculated on your turnover (total amount wagered), not your net loss.

Real Example — RTPPokies 0.9% Daily Rebate

Say you bet AUD$1,000 in a day on pokies at RTPPokies. At 0.9% rebate:

  • Total wagered: AUD$1,000
  • Rebate: AUD$1,000 × 0.9% = AUD$9 cash back

That might sound small, but if you're a regular player betting AUD$5,000/week, that's AUD$45/week or AUD$2,340/year just for playing normally.

RTPMeth 0.8% Daily Rollover

RTPMeth calculates and pays your rebate daily. It hits your account every 24 hours automatically — no claiming required.

RTPCunt 5% Weekly Rebate

This one is based on net losses over the week. If you lose AUD$200 in a week, you get AUD$10 back every Monday. It stacks up fast for active players.
